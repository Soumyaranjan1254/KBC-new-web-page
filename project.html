Information of Kalinga Bikash Computers

Kalinga Bikash Computers is a premier organization in the field of Computer education & provide computer education to more than 2,00,000 students till today. Kalinga Bikash is a voluntary organization involved in serving people for betterment.



Established & other Informations

Established on 29th Feb. 1996in the objective to spread the latest forms of education and many other schemes for the upliftment of the weaker sections of the society. As a part of ongoing process to serve the mass the society had came forward to offer different types of computer courses to allthe aspiring youngsters and for all of them who really have the urge to learn. KBC providing both software and hardware course by its all branches in Odisha. Besides the traking, KBC is also providing Placement Assistance to the students.
